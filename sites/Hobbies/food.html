Nam Tok Neua

Nam tok Neua is a famous Thai dish, which translate into waterfall beef. Nam tok neua consist of slices grill beef, toasted rice powders, fish sauce, sugars, Thai chili peppers, lime juice, green onion, cilantro, and mint leaves. Then you top it off with lettuce or cabbage with a bowl of sticky rice. Try it out when you guys can.

Pho

Pho is a famous noodle soup dish originate from southeast Asia. I eat pho for breakfast, lunch, and dinner. I have to eat this dish at least once a week. There are different ways to make pho but the secret is with the broth. Your pho taste as how you make the broth, so make sure you triple check the broth.
