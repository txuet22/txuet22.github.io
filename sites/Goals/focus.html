Another Goal

I want to spent more time with my families

One my other goal is to spend time with my families. First, I want to spend more time with my two little boys and wife. Being a full-time employee and student, making time is hard. Everyday my boys would ask their mom, "where is daddy going?" This breaks my heart to hear them say that, so I decided to made them my priority when i can. Some of my goal set for my boys are to take them to the pumpkin patch, cook them dinners, spend time shopping with them on holiday gifts, and etc. I also want to record all of my events with them to show myself at the end of the year. I even bought a tripod to hold my phone up while recording. Before my dad pass away of 2011, my siblings and I was not close. We hardly talk to each other or do family events as a family. Now, we are closer since the passing of my father, but I do wish to spend more time with them. Life is so short, spend time with the people that you want to be with. Just in a instance and it will all be gone. I just want to live my life to the fullest.
