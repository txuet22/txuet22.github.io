St. Paul, Minnesota

My love for St. Paul, Minnesota is as great as baby eating a bottle. This city just rip my heart away when I first arrive there. The city host the biggest hmong volleyball sport tournament, it as the biggest mall in the USA: Mall of America, and also home to the NFL team Minnesota Vikings. My biggest memory will always be taking 1st place there in 2016. We had to play all the best teams to finish first place. There was no easy way to the finals. They have over 50+ teams that register to play every year. Everytime I'm in St. Paul, I feel like a winner. The Mall of America is like the biggest mall ever. They even have a rollercoaster ride inside the mall. The state don't tax, so buying things there is a little cheaper. The famous viking chant, skol is from St. Paul, MN. The fans of Minnesota are truely die hard fan, and I am one of them. Skol!Skol!
