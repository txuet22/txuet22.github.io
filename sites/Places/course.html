Detroit, Michigan

Detroit had to be my first out of state trip. Will when I went there, it wasn't really meant for a trip. I was about 16 years old, when I first flew on a plane. My sister and brother-in-law just had their second baby and needed a babysitter. So over that summer, they bought me a ticket to Detroit. I was blown away when I first arrived there. In the first view street of Detroit city, you will see true poverty there but once you slowing go drive up north, you see the suburbs of Detroit. Popular rappers like Eminem and 50 cent are from Detroit, MI. The city has more then it looks, you just have to give it a opportunity.

Lake Huron

One of the four great lakes. Lake Huron is located on the eastern side of Michigan. It was about 1hr drive from Detroit to Lake Huron. I came remember as it was yesterday when I first stop the lakeside of Lake Huron. The lake was so beautiful and it wasn't pack like the California beaches. I would love to visit Lake Huron again.

Troy Mall

Troy Mall is about 25 mins north west from Detroit. It is a multiple level mall and was my first mall I went that had more than two levels. I remember the mall a big water area. The mall itself, isn't so different as other mall, but the location of the mall is. It is located next to the Detroit Piston Arena. When I was there, it was the year that the Piston won the NBA Championship.
